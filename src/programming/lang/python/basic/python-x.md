## Python2 vs Python3

| Python2   | Python3 
| ---       | ---       
| `print`   | `print()` 
| `xrange()`| `range()` 
| `except IOError, (errno, strerror)` | `except IOError as (errno, strerror)` 
| `3 / 2 = 1` | `3 / 2 = 1.5`
